Helpline Numbers

In case of emergency: If you are in immediate danger, please call your local emergency services (e.g., 911, 112, 100). The numbers below are primarily US-based or global directories.
  • 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (US): Call or text 988.
  • Crisis Text Line (US, UK, CA, IE): Text HOME to 741741.
  • SAMHSA National Helpline (US): Call 1-800-662-4357. (Substance Abuse & Mental Health)
  • National Domestic Violence Hotline (US): Call 1-800-799-7233 or text START to 88788.
  • The Trevor Project (US): Call 1-866-488-7386 or text START to 678-678. (LGBTQ+ Youth)
  • Childhelp National Child Abuse Hotline (US): Call 1-800-422-4453.
  • NEDA Helpline (US): Call or text 1-800-931-2237. (Eating Disorders)
  • Veterans Crisis Line (US): Call 988 and Press 1, or text 838255.
  • Global Directory (Befrienders Worldwide): Provides a directory for crisis support in most countries.
  • Global Directory (IASP): International Association for Suicide Prevention global crisis center directory.
  • NCMEC (US): Call 1-800-THE-LOST (1-800-843-5678). (National Center for Missing & Exploited Children)
